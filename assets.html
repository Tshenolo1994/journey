It became clear that their request had not been relayed to the Chief at all. As incentive to deliver the message, the brothers offered more cattle to the Ga-Ngwetsana clan in the hopes that this time their request would be relayed timeously. It was not. Having realised that the Ga-Ngwetsana clan had no real intent to relay their request to the Chief, it became clear to the brothers that they would have to find another way of gaining an audience with the Chief to have their request for land heard
